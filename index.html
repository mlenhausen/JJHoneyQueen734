JJ HoneyQueen734

JJ Furlong AKA JJ HoneyQueen734 is a singer-songwriter from Ann Arbor, Michigan. A scientist by nature, her writing is based on observations in her own life, nature, and the experiences of those around her. Her lyrics combine poetry, simplicity, and vulnerability creating a universal, timeless feel. JJ creates Pop music that draws from Rock, RnB, and Soul influences. Known for her magnetic stage presence and soulful voice, the best way to experience her music is in a live setting.
